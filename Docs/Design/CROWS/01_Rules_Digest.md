# CROWS TCG — Rules Digest (from "CROWS TCG - Rules Concept.docx")

Champion duel TCG with TTRPG combat: d20 strikes vs Armor Class, dice damage,
boons/ailments, 3 actions per turn. "Bring RPG combat to a TCG table."

## Core loop
- Deck: champion + cards (doc says both "20 cards and a Champion" and
  "21 cards, being 19 plus the Champion" — **needs a ruling**, see Gaps).
- Max 3 copies per card; UNIQUE cards max 1. Champion has TWO Aspects that
  define which cards the deck may include, plus universal Items.
- Turn-zero: both players deploy champion + deployment cards.
- Each turn: **draw 4**, get **3 actions**; cards cost actions to play;
  pass when done; **discard the whole remaining hand at end of turn**.
- Empty deck → shuffle discard into a new deck (built-in recycle).
- Champion = player. Champion at 0 Life = loss.

## Battlefield (per player)
- 1 Champion slot
- 2 Hand/Equip slots (equipment; playing a 3rd replaces one)
- 3 Field slots (continuous / temporary cards; minions live here)
- Deck, Discard (public), **Consumed zone** (removed from game, public)

## Resolution
- **Strike**: d20 + bonuses vs target's ARMOR (AC). >= hits.
  Nat 20 = crit (roll damage dice twice). Nat 1 = critical failure —
  **CONSUME the card**.
- **Saving**: d20 + bonuses vs 10 (when unspecified).
- Damage/effects use dice: d4/d6/d8/d10/d12/d20.
- **Advantage/Disadvantage**: roll twice take best/worst (5e-style).
- **Prepare**: pay 2 actions, card sits face-down until its trigger (traps).
- **Temporary** field cards last 3 of your turns; **Continuous** until removed.
- **Sacrifice**: send own field card (not champion) to discard.
- **Search**: tutor any card, reveal, shuffle.

## Boons
Sharpened (next hit +d4), Advantage, Blessed (adv on next save/strike),
Empowered (target of next save-card has disadvantage), Enraged (adv strikes
both ways), Focused (cards cost 1 less action), Hidden (strikes vs you have
disadvantage until you act), Hunting (next strike adv), Inspired (+1 all
rolls), Lucky (failed save → reroll w/ adv, once), Quickened (+1 action).

## Ailments
Bleeding (d4 stacks; extra dmg when damaged, -1/proc), Burning (d6 stacks;
dmg at end of opponent turn, -1/round), Chilled (first card +1 cost — doc
says "mana", see Gaps), Cursed (draw 1 less), Dazed (max actions -1),
Disadvantage, Distress (all cards +1 action), Entangled (disadv on
strikes/saves; spend 1 action to clear), Poisoned (d4 stacks; dmg at start
of opponent turn, -1/round), Sleeping (lose action or discard 2 random),
Smitten (next strike vs you +d4), Stunned (lose 2 actions), Trauma
(opponent discards 1 random card from your hand, once).

## The 10 Aspects
Might (warriors) · Heat (fire/fury) · Prospect (gold/economy) ·
Divine (light/deities) · Gale (storm/mind) · Land (hunt/nature)** ·
Boreal (water/ice)** · Arcana (cosmos/pure magic)** · Dusk (deception) ·
Occult (pacts/dark arts).  (** marked in source, meaning unclear.)

## Gaps / rulings needed (from the doc as written)
1. Deck size: 20 cards + champion, or 19 + champion (=20 total)?
2. "Chilled" says +1 **mana** — actions and mana used interchangeably?
   (Assume actions?)
3. Do MINIONS occupy the 3 field slots (shared with continuous cards)? Doc
   implies yes — so max 3 permanents of any kind per side?
4. Who initiates strikes and with what — do attack cards strike from hand
   (action cost, resolve, discard) while equipment strikes repeatedly?
5. Champion base stats (Life/AC) — per-champion printed values; typical range?
6. Nat-1 CONSUME on equipment strikes: consume the equipment itself?
7. Saving throws: who rolls — the defender always? DC always 10 unless stated?
8. Hand limit? (Discard-at-end makes it moot except mid-turn effects.)
9. Are strikes an action themselves, or free after playing the card?
